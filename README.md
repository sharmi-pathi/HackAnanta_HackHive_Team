->Campus Mental Health Chatbot

A web-based mental health support application for college students that provides emotional assistance through an AI-powered chatbot and mood tracking.

->Problem

Students face stress, anxiety, and loneliness but often lack immediate and stigma-free support.

->Solution

An AI chatbot that helps students express emotions, track moods, and reflect on their mental well-being in a safe digital space.

->Features

Secure Login & Signup

AI Chatbot using Google Dialogflow

Mood Tracking & Visualization

Reflection / Chat History Library

Clean and user-friendly interface

->Technologies Used

Google Dialogflow – Chatbot AI

Firebase Authentication – User login

Firebase Firestore – Data storage

Firebase Hosting – Live deployment

HTML, CSS, JavaScript – Frontend
